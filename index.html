Who I Am

About Umair Ahmad

A detail-driven Mechanical BIM Engineer with real international project experience, strong simulation background, and technical depth GCC employers look for.

Professional Profile

I am a Mechanical BIM Engineer and MEP Revit Modeler & Detailer with professional experience at BIMTEQ, serving American clients in Houston USA. I model, coordinate, and detail full MEP systems — HVAC ducting, chilled water, ventilation, and plumbing — at LOD 400–450.

HVAC Ducting & Piping

Specialized in HVAC Ducting Modeling & Detailing and Piping Systems Design using Revit MEP. I develop accurate 3D duct layouts, piping networks (chilled water, supply, drainage), and coordinate all systems to eliminate clashes before construction.

BIM Coordination & Clash Detection

Using Navisworks, I perform full multidisciplinary BIM coordination — identifying and resolving clashes between mechanical, electrical, plumbing, architectural, and structural models. I produce clash detection reports and coordinate with all discipline teams.

Simulation & Engineering Analysis

Beyond BIM — strong skills in SolidWorks (Advanced: 3D modeling, FEA), ANSYS (Advanced: structural, thermal, CFD/CFX), ABAQUS (Intermediate: explicit dynamics), and COMSOL Multiphysics (Intermediate: multiphase CFD).
